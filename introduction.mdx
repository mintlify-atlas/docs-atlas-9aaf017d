The platform works across multiple mine sites under one organization, with role-based access so each team member sees exactly what they need.

## Key capabilities

- **Multi-site management** — one organization account, unlimited mine sites, each with its own data and team
- **Offline-first** — record transactions, update inventory, and log production even without internet; changes sync automatically when you reconnect
- **Role-based access** — assign admin, site manager, worker, or viewer roles per site
- **Configurable modules** — enable or disable Messages, Campaigns, Customers, Reports, Team, Supply Chain, and Operations modules per organization
- **Automated alerts** — set KPI targets and inventory reorder thresholds; the platform notifies you when thresholds are crossed
- **Audit trail** — every change is recorded in the audit log for compliance and review
