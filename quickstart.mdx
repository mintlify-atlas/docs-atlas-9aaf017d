FW Mining OS sets up in a single session. By the end of this guide you will have a working organization, at least one mine site, your first team member invited, and your first transaction on record.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to the **Register** page in FW Mining OS and fill in the sign-up form:

    - **Organization name** — the legal or trading name of your mining company. This becomes your top-level tenant and cannot be changed after registration.
    - **Your full name**
    - **Email address**
    - **Password** — minimum 8 characters

    After you submit, FW Mining OS automatically creates your organization, a default site called **Main Site**, and assigns you the **admin** role for that site.

    <Note>
      If your organization already has an account, do not register a second one. Ask your admin to invite you via the Team section instead.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first site">
    After logging in you land on the Dashboard. By default, registration creates a **Main Site** for you. Rename it or add additional sites to match your real mine locations:

    1. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
    2. Go to **Organization**.
    3. Under **Sites**, click **Add site**.
    4. Enter a **Site name**, **Location**, and **Timezone**.
    5. Click **Save**.

    The new site appears in the site selector at the top of the sidebar. Switch between sites at any time — all data (transactions, inventory, team) is scoped to the active site.

    <Tip>
      Use descriptive names like "Kalgoorlie North Pit" rather than generic labels so team members can identify sites at a glance.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Bring your team in without sharing passwords. FW Mining OS uses invite links so each member registers their own credentials.

    1. Select the site you want to add members to using the site selector.
    2. Click **Team** in the left sidebar.
    3. Click **Invite member**.
    4. Enter the member's **email address** and select their **role** (`site_manager`, `worker`, or `viewer`).
    5. Click **Send invite**.

    The invitee receives an email with a link to the **Accept Invite** page. They set their name and password, and are immediately added to the site with the role you assigned.

    <Note>
      Roles are per-site. If a user needs access to multiple sites, invite them to each site separately with the appropriate role for each.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure modules">
    FW Mining OS ships with all feature modules enabled. If your organization does not use a module — for example, if you have no customer-facing sales — you can disable it to keep the interface clean.

    1. Click **Settings** → **System**.
    2. Toggle off any modules your organization does not need: **Messages**, **Campaigns**, **Customers**, **Reports**, **Team**, **Supply Chain**, or **Operations**.
    3. Changes take effect immediately for all users in your organization.

    <Tip>
      You can re-enable any module at any time. Disabling a module hides its navigation items and pages but does not delete any data.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Record your first transaction">
    Transactions are the financial backbone of each site. Record an income or expense entry to confirm everything is working.

    1. Click **Transactions** in the left sidebar.
    2. Click **New Transaction**.
    3. Fill in the form:
       - **Type** — `income`, `expense`, or `refund`
       - **Amount** — unit price × quantity
       - **Category** — e.g., `fuel`, `equipment`, `gold_sale`
       - **Date** — defaults to today
       - **Description** (optional) — a short note for reference
    4. Click **Save**.

    The transaction appears in the list immediately and is reflected in the Dashboard KPIs.

    <Note>
      FW Mining OS is offline-first. If you are not connected to the internet when you save, the transaction is queued locally and syncs automatically the next time you come online.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your Dashboard">
    Return to the Dashboard (click the FW Mining OS logo or navigate to `/`) to see your data reflected in real time.

    The KPI cards at the top of the Dashboard show:

    - **Revenue** — total income transactions for the selected period
    - **Expenses** — total expense transactions for the selected period
    - **Net Profit** — revenue minus expenses
    - **Trend indicators** — period-over-period change for each metric

    Use the period selector to switch between daily, weekly, monthly, and custom ranges.

    <Tip>
      Set KPI targets in **Settings → Targets** so the Dashboard highlights when revenue or profit falls below your goals.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

</Steps>
